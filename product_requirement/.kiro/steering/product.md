# FemCare — Product Steering

## What is FemCare?

FemCare is an AI-assisted gynaecological health platform that maximises the clinical value of the annual appointment — before, during, and after the consultation. It is a B2B2C SaaS product licensed to gynaecology practices, women's health clinics, and hospital networks.

## Core Problem

The annual gynaecological checkup is structurally broken:
- Appointments average 12–15 minutes but must cover cervical screening, STI testing, contraception, breast health, and mental health
- Patients arrive unprepared — no structured symptom history
- No year-on-year continuity — each visit starts from scratch
- Up to 40% of follow-up actions are never completed

## The Three-Phase Model

### Phase 1: Pre-Visit
- Patient completes a structured intake form: current symptoms, reproductive history, family history, lifestyle factors, cycle data
- AI triages responses and flags red-alert symptoms for urgent escalation
- Auto-generates a concise, structured patient brief delivered to the clinician before the appointment
- Integrates with cycle tracking apps where patient consents

### Phase 2: In-Consultation
- Clinician sees patient brief and longitudinal history summary
- AI surfaces a dynamic screening checklist adapted to patient age and risk profile
- Real-time lab result interpretation with year-on-year trend comparison
- Decision support only — AI never replaces or overrides clinician judgment
- All AI suggestions must be clearly labelled as advisory

### Phase 3: Post-Visit
- Patient receives a plain-language summary of findings and next steps
- Automated follow-up reminder system with AI escalation for missed actions
- Longitudinal health timeline — builds a 10+ year health story
- Optional wearable integration for between-visit monitoring

## Users

### Primary: Patients
- Women aged 20–60
- Mobile-first experience
- Non-clinical language throughout
- Varying levels of health literacy — always explain, never assume

### Secondary: Clinicians
- Gynaecologists and GPs
- Desktop and tablet
- Clinical language acceptable
- Workflow must reduce friction, not add it — every extra click is a problem

### Tertiary: Practice Administrators
- Scheduling, billing, reporting
- Desktop

## Business Model

- B2B2C — licensed to clinics and hospital networks
- €150–300 per clinician seat per month
- EHR integration (Epic first, then Nexus, Medidata)
- Long-term insurance reimbursement pathway (DRG)

## Customer Segments

| Segment | Value proposition |
|---------|------------------|
| Gynaecology practices | Reduce prep time, increase care quality, fewer liability gaps |
| Women's health clinics | Differentiated offering, attract data-literate patients |
| Hospital networks | Population screening analytics, early detection ROI |
| Health insurers | Preventive care data reduces downstream claims |

## Competitive Moat

- Longitudinal patient record — value compounds every year
- EHR integration creates deep switching cost
- No specialty-specific AI tool dominates gynaecology yet
- Clinician trust built via proven, non-intrusive decision support

## Non-Negotiable Principles

1. **AI is advisory only.** No clinical decision is ever automated. Every AI output must be reviewable and dismissable by a clinician.
2. **Privacy by design.** Patient data is never used for advertising, never sold, never shared without explicit consent.
3. **Plain language for patients.** Medical jargon must always be translated into accessible language in patient-facing surfaces.
4. **Auditability.** Every AI recommendation and every data access event must be logged for regulatory review.
5. **Do not over-engineer.** Start with the pre-visit module. Prove value before adding complexity.
